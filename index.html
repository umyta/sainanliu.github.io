About

I’m a Staff AI Research Scientist at Intel Labs working on 3D reconstruction/generation and video generation. I collaborate across research and product teams, mentor students, and publish at venues including CVPR, ICML, WACV, and ICRA.

Research Interests

  • Video diffusion and controllable video editing
  • Neural radiance fields & graphics primitives
  • Perception for robotics
